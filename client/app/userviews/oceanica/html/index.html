Where to Eat

The Best in the World

Our area is home to some wonderful eateries and delicious foods you won't find anywhere else. We're excited to share some of our favorites with you.



RUMPUS is our absolute favorite tapas place here! The patatas bravas are done to perfection, served with a delicious homemade hollandaise. If Daniella is there, tell her hi from us!

EARLY is a beautiful cafe, with full views of the city. If you get there before 7, they serve omelettes made to-order.

Don't miss the crepes at MS. SUMMER'S. Once you've had one, it's hard to stop going back.
